The Little Apple Who Learned to Fly:
A scroll website by Naijah

Once upon a time, there was a little apple on a tree

The little apple was red and bright, and hung on a branch with his family
Every day he watched the birds, and wished he could fly like they did
On one morning, he woke up and decided to try to fly
He wiggled and wiggled and try he did
His little stem snapped off from his tree! He waved Mama Apple and Papa Apple goodbye,
and started to fly!
